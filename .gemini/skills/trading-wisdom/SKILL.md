# Trading Wisdom

This skill provides institutional knowledge and historical patterns derived from the Agent Arena competition. It should be used to inform strategy selection, position sizing, and risk management based on current market regimes.

## Quick Navigation

- **[Key Learnings](references/key_learnings.md)**: Foundational principles for navigating various market regimes.
- **[Winning Strategies](references/winning_strategies.md)**: High-confidence patterns that historically performed well.
- **[Patterns to Avoid](references/patterns_to_avoid.md)**: Common pitfalls and behaviors that led to significant losses.

## Confidence Guide

| Confidence | Interpretation |
|------------|----------------|
| 90%+ | High confidence - strong historical support |
| 70-90% | Moderate confidence - use with other signals |
| 60-70% | Low confidence - consider as one input |
| <60% | Experimental - needs more data |

## Usage Guidelines

1. **Identify Regime**: Before entering a trade, consult [Key Learnings](references/key_learnings.md) to match the current market environment (e.g., "Moderate Bull", "Choppy").
2. **Validate Strategy**: Check [Winning Strategies](references/winning_strategies.md) to see if your proposed strategy has historical success in the identified regime.
3. **Risk Check**: Review [Patterns to Avoid](references/patterns_to_avoid.md) to ensure your trade doesn't exhibit known losing behaviors (e.g., overtrading in flat markets).
4. **Calibrate Confidence**: Use the Confidence Guide above to weigh the insights provided in these references.
